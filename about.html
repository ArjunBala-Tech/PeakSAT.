ABOUT PEAKSAT

Created by a student, for students.

PeakSAT was built to make SAT prep feel clearer, more modern, and more useful.

ABOUT ME

Arjun Balasubramanian

I am a high school junior, an aspiring software engineer, and a member of the Class of 2027 at Stephen F. Austin High School.

WHY I MADE THIS

A better SAT experience

I wanted a site that explains the SAT in a way that actually helps students, instead of just overwhelming them with practice questions immediately.

GOAL

Make prep less confusing

The goal is to help students feel informed, confident, and better prepared through stronger design, better explanations, and better structure.
